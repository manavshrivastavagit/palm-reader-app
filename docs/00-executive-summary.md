## Vision

PalmVerse is an AI-powered palmistry platform that combines cutting-edge computer vision, multi-tradition palmistry knowledge (Vedic, Western, Chinese), and conversational AI to deliver the most accurate, personalized, and immersive palm reading experience ever built. It transforms the ancient art of chiromancy into a modern, data-driven wellness companion.

## Why Now?

1. **Technology Maturity** — Computer vision (MediaPipe, CNNs) can now trace palm lines with 80–90% accuracy via smartphone cameras
2. **Cultural Shift** — Spiritual wellness is mainstream; Gen Z uses astrology/palmistry as a self-discovery and mental health tool
3. **AI Conversational Leap** — LLMs with RAG architecture enable deeply personalized, context-aware readings grounded in real palmistry traditions
4. **Market Gap** — No single app combines best-in-class CV scanning + multi-tradition knowledge + AI chat + live experts + social community

## Business Model

**Hybrid Monetization:** Freemium + Subscription + IAP + Ads

- **Free Tier:** 1 scan/day, basic line analysis, daily affirmations
- **Premium Subscription:** $7.99/mo or $49.99/yr — unlimited scans, AI chat, detailed reports, ad-free
- **IAP:** Deep-dive reports ($2.99–$9.99), expert consultations ($14.99–$29.99/session)
- **Ads:** Rewarded video ads for free users to unlock bonus scans
